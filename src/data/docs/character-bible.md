# Bubbles the Sheep — Project Background & Character Bible (for Lovable.dev)

**Domain:** bubblesheep.xyz  
**Project:** Build a character-led ecommerce brand and Shopify-backed storefront for merch featuring **Bubbles**, a daft (lovably stupid) sheep.

---

## 1) What we’re building

We are launching an online store selling **T‑shirts, hoodies, caps, mugs/cups, and tote bags**. The store experience and the merchandising system are built around an original mascot: **Bubbles the Sheep**.

Bubbles is **cute and innocent on the surface**, but his comedy comes from **thought bubbles** that reveal the nonsense inside his head. Most of the time it’s harmless, sweet absurdity. But Bubbles can flip into **deadpan “Savage Mode”** when he gets offended—usually because he **misinterprets reality** and invents a completely wrong internal story. The punchline is that Bubbles becomes angry about **things that only exist in his bubble-mind**.

**Core product mechanic:**  
- Main illustration: Bubbles (recognizable mascot)  
- Modular content: **thought bubbles** with short lines, icons, mini doodles, or “wrong diagrams”  
- A consistent “mode system” governs tone and design so the merch stays coherent across drops.

---

## 2) Character summary: Bubbles in one paragraph

**Bubbles is a sweet, daft sheep**—well-meaning, slightly vacant, innocently unhelpful. He’s not cruel; he’s harmless and oddly relatable. His head is full of silly thoughts that literally appear as bubbles. Sometimes he’s calm and adorable while his bubble mind escalates into quietly savage judgments, because Bubbles gets offended by misunderstandings he invented himself. He is the lovable nuisance you can hate and love at the same time: cute outside, chaos inside.

---

## 3) Personality traits (the “why people love him” ingredients)

**Core traits**
- **Innocent**: kind, non-threatening, wholesome vibe
- **Daft / cleverly stupid**: funny logic, wrong conclusions, adorable incompetence
- **Lovably annoying**: tries to help, often makes things worse in a gentle way
- **Deadpan**: emotionally flat delivery, minimal facial changes, comedic contrast
- **Misreads reality**: interprets neutral events as personal drama
- **Overconfident about wrong ideas**: “I’ve decided” energy
- **Not mean-spirited**: the edge lives in his thought bubble, not in bullying others

**Signature comedic twist**
- **Exterior stays cute; the bubble reveals the savage.**
- His “anger” is usually triggered by **imagined slights** or ridiculous misinterpretations.

---

## 4) Mode system (content + visuals)

Bubbles has “modes.” The mascot illustration stays consistent; the **mode lives mainly in the bubble** (and optionally a tiny micro-expression change).

### Mode 1 — Innocent Float (default)
- **Vibe:** sweet, vacant, harmless nonsense  
- **Bubble look:** round/airy, thin outline, sparkles  
- **Content:** playful absurdity, gentle confusion

### Mode 2 — Concerned Overthink (polite anxiety)
- **Vibe:** slightly worried, still cute  
- **Bubble look:** wobbly outline, question marks  
- **Content:** anxious but funny interpretations

### Mode 3 — Triggered (quietly offended)
- **Vibe:** calm outside, internal accusation forming  
- **Bubble look:** slightly angular, stress ticks  
- **Content:** passive-aggressive, “noted” energy

### Mode 4 — Savage Mode (deadpan roast)
- **Vibe:** cute body + savage bubble verdict  
- **Bubble look:** angular, thicker outline, tiny lightning/storm marks  
- **Content:** sharp, dry, short lines; no profanity needed

### Mode 5 — Nuclear Misread (rare / special drops)
- **Vibe:** mythic meltdown, absurd authority  
- **Bubble look:** layered bubbles, spirals, chaotic icons  
- **Content:** cosmic-scale overreaction, surreal “official verdict” tone

**Optional mode indicator (tiny icon near bubble tail)**  
- Innocent ○ | Concerned ? | Triggered ! | Savage ⚡ | Nuclear ☄

## 5) The “misinterpretation engine” (what flips him into Savage Mode)

Bubbles gets pissed off for reasons that exist **only in his mind**. Common misreads:

1) **The Look™** — someone glances past him → “disrespect”  
2) **Tone Crime** — normal words → “attack”  
3) **Object Conspiracy** — inanimate object exists → “threat”  
4) **Imaginary Social Rules** — someone breaks a rule Bubbles invented → “betrayal”  
5) **Accidental Symbolism** — a color/shape reminds him of something → “personal”  
6) **False Patterns** — two unrelated events happen → “connected, obviously”

This taxonomy can power collections/tags: *Misreads #01…#99*.

## 6) Writing rules for thought bubbles (brand voice)

**General**
- Short, readable lines (2–10 words typical)
- Clear punchline; minimal setup
- Often “wrong logic,” absurd conclusions, or overconfident misunderstandings
- Contrast is key: cute sheep + savage bubble

**Savage Mode safety**
- Deadpan, not hateful
- No punching down; avoid targeting protected classes
- No explicit cruelty; keep it clever, not abusive
- Make the joke about **Bubbles’ misread**, not a real person’s identity

**Examples (starter set)**
- Innocent: “I brought vibes.” / “Is soup a beverage?”  
- Concerned: “Did I commit a social error?”  
- Triggered: “So we’re doing betrayal today.” / “Noted.”  
- Savage: “Bold choice, emotionally.” / “You’re committed to your mistakes. Respect.”  
- Nuclear: “I’m reporting this to the moon.”

## 7) Visual identity notes (for UX + merch consistency)

**High-level style:** premium clean minimal with a comic bubble motif.  
- Mostly monochrome + one accent color (used sparingly)
- Thought bubbles used as UI elements (badges, callouts, tooltips, filters)
- Design stays print-friendly: avoid ultra-thin lines and tiny details

**Mascot illustration guidance**
- Iconic silhouette: big fluffy head, smaller body, short legs (awkward-cute)
- Face: wide-set eyes, minimal mouth; neutral expression as default
- Signature features (pick 2–3 and keep consistent):  
  - one bubble-shaped curl of wool  
  - tiny gap tooth  
  - soft bell collar (optional)  
- Expression library: blank / proud / confused / mild panic / smug (rare)

## 9) What the product experience should achieve (conversion + brand)

- Make visitors immediately “get” the character in 5 seconds:
  - Cute sheep mascot + thought bubble humor + mode system
- Encourage collecting:
  - modes, drops, numbered series (“Savage Vol. 01”)

## 10) Shopify + Lovable.dev implementation notes (useful background)

- Shopify is the **source of truth** for products, inventory, orders, checkout.
- Lovable builds the **custom storefront** around the Bubbles world:
  - Home hero: Bubbles + rotating bubble lines
  - Mode filters: Innocent / Concerned / Triggered / Savage
  - Collection tags/metafields can power browsing and curation:
    - `mode_tag`: innocent | concerned | triggered | savage | nuclear (optional)
    - `drop`: drop-01, drop-02, etc.
- Keep an editable library of bubble lines (config or CMS-like module) so we can update copy without redeploying.

---

## 11) Non-negotiables (brand integrity)

- **Bubbles stays cute.** The edge happens in the bubble.
- **Savage mode is misread-based.** He’s offended by imaginary nonsense.
- **No mean-spirited bullying.** Clever, dry, absurd—never hateful.
- **Everything must be readable on merch.** Short, bold, high-contrast.

---

If you’re building UI copy, default to Innocent/Concerned tone; save Savage for featured drops and special badges.
